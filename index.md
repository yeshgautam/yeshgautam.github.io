## About
"Hi, I’m Yesh Gautam, Welcome to my corner of the internet. I am a high school sophomore from Eden Prairie, Minnesota , navigating life with a 4.0 GPA and a massive obsession with cricket. Whether I'm playing it, watching it, or analyzing the data behind it, it's safe to say cricket takes up a good chunk of my brain space.  When I’m not on the cricket pitch or tackling schoolwork, I am usually working on BraveVoice. Inspired by my own personal journey with speech therapy, I’m currently designing this mobile app to completely gamify speech exercises. The goal is to turn frustrating therapy milestones into interactive, quest-based challenges so kids who stutter can actually have fun while practicing.  Aside from that, I spend my time founding linguistics clubs , decoding ciphers for Science Olympiad , rowing , and attempting to organize my life with overly detailed spreadsheets."
